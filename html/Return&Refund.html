Returns & Refunds

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, you can return your items within 30 days of receiving them.

Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with tags attached.

Please allow 5-7 business days for your return to be processed once it arrives at our facility.

Return Request Form

Processing Details

Once we receive your return, please allow the following timeline for processing:

  • Return Processing: 5-7 business days.
  • Refund Issued: 3-5 business days after return is processed.
  • Notification: You will receive an email notification once your return is processed.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the shipping options available?

We offer standard shipping, expedited shipping, and free shipping on orders over $50. Delivery times may vary based on your location.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of receipt. Items must be unworn and in original packaging. Please visit our Returns page for more details.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your order on our website using the tracking number provided.

What should I do if I receive a defective item?

If you receive a defective item, please contact our customer service within 7 days of receiving your order for a replacement or refund.

How do I find my size?

We provide a size guide on each product page. Please refer to it for accurate sizing information. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up.
